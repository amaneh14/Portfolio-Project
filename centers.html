High Velocity Sports

High Velocity Sports opened in December 2001 and has become Canton's premier indoor sports complex. Located in Victory Park in front of the Canton Softball Center and next to the Arctic Edge, the 110,000 square-foot facility offers several sports, including indoor soccer, flag football, dodge ball, basketball, and volleyball. Clinics for soccer, basketball, and volleyball are also available along with summer and school break camps. Birthday parties, field trips, lock-ins, corporate outings, and team building are other options.
